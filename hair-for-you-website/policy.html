Refund & Return Policy

All sales are final on hair, wigs, and beauty products due to hygiene reasons. Exchanges only available if the wrong item was sent, unopened and returned within 48 hours.

Booking Rules

A non-refundable deposit is required to hold appointments. Arrive on time. Cancellations must be made 24 hours in advance.

Shipping & Delivery

Processing time 1–2 business days. Local delivery in Nassau may be same/next day. Family Islands delivery 1–3 business days. Delivery fees vary by location.
